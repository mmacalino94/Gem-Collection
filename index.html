Video Game Treasure Hunter

How To Play


"Goal" : Random Number given to you at the start of the game

These are the four gamer currency items. Each of them will have a different currency value

  • Sonic Ring
  • Wumpa Fruit
  • Mario Coin
  • Hyrule Red Rupee

Click on a gamer currency item to add your total score points.

Win: Match your total score to the "Goal"

Lose: If your score is higher than the Goal

The value of each currency item is hidden from you until you click on it and will change to a random number in every round.
