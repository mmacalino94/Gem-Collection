Video Game Treasure Hunter

Instructions


  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game."Number To Get"
  2. There are four video game currency items below.
    • Chaos Emerald
    • Wumpa Fruit
    • Coin
    • Red Rupee
  3. By clicking on a video game currency item, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  4. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.
  5. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  6. The value of each currency item is hidden from you until you click on it.
  7. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each video game currecnty item.
